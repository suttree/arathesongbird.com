Ara the Songbird

A calming robot companion for life.

Ara is a lo-fi a.i. who generates their own birdsong and sings multiple times throughout each day. And just like a real bird, Ara will tweet a little bit more at sunrise and sunset.

There are a limited number of kits ready to fly, and no technical knowledge is needed to assemble them.

What you get

When you purchase you’ll receive an arduino kit that requires no assembly or internet connection. Your ara kit includes:

  • one arduino
  • one speaker
  • one real-time clock chip
  • a 3d printed case
  • a hand-carved friendly bird
